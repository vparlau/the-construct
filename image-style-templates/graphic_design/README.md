# Graphic Design Styles

This category contains image generation prompt templates for graphic design, print graphics, and poster art styles.

## What belongs here

- Vector and flat design styles
- Poster art and print graphics
- Typography-heavy designs
- Street art and graffiti styles
- Technical and blueprint styles
- Printmaking techniques (woodcut, etching, lino print)
- Abstract digital art styles

## Styles included

- Vector / Flat Design
- Minimalism / Flat Illustrative
- Retro / Vintage Poster
- Pop Art / Comic
- Bauhaus / Constructivist
- Vaporwave / Synthwave
- Graffiti / Street Art
- Chalkboard / Chalk Art
- Propaganda Poster
- Blueprint / Technical
- Woodcut / Lino Print
- Etching / Engraving
- Doodle / Zentangle
- Glitch / Abstract Digital Art
