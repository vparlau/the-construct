# Painting Styles

This category contains image generation prompt templates for traditional and historical painting styles.

## What belongs here

- Traditional painting techniques (oil, watercolor, ink wash)
- Historical art movements and periods
- Painterly styles that emphasize brushwork and artistic technique

## Styles included

- Traditional Painting
- Impressionism / Expressionism
- Ink and Wash
- Watercolor Painting
- Cubist Composition
- Futurist Dynamics
- Gothic / Baroque
- Rococo
