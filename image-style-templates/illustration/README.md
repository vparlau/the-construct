# Illustration Styles

This category contains image generation prompt templates for various illustration styles, both digital and traditional.

## What belongs here

- Digital and traditional illustration techniques
- Character and narrative illustration styles
- Specialized illustration genres (botanical, medical, fashion, children's book)
- Fantasy and sci-fi illustration styles
- Anime, manga, and cartoon styles
